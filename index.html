Terminology

1. Quicklinks

Quicklinks refer to the links that you have saved to the extension that you want to quickly access from the extension panel. Click on the Quicklink to open the URL in a new tab.

2. Workspaces

Workspaces are the collection of Quicklinks that belong in the same group. For example, you can create workspaces for things like Daily for your daily usage, Socials for your social media handles, etc.

3. Options page

Options page is the extension's configuration page where you can configure Quicklinks to your needs. Access the options page by clicking on the 'Settings icon' in the extension panel.

How to use the Quicklinks extension

  1. After downloading Quicklinks, head over to the options page by clicking on the icon, besides the Quicklinks logo in the extension panel.
  2. To create a new workspace, click on the on the options page, and select 'Create New Workspace'.
  3. Enter the name of the new workspace and a description for the new workspace. Then submit the form to create your new workspace.
  4. Select your newly created workspace for the dropdown.
  5. To add a new Quicklink to your workspace, click on the again, and select 'Create New Quicklink'.
  6. In the modal, select the workspace to which you would like to add your new Quicklink. Then, provide your Quicklink's Name, URL and Description, and submit to create your new Quicklink.
  7. All your Quicklinks for a particular workspace can be viewed by selecting the workspace from the dropdown.
